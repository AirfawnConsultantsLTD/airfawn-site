About Us

Airfawn Ltd is a family-run business with over 25 years of experience in the auto electrical industry. We are specialists in vehicle wiring harness manufacturing and provide trusted service across the UK and overseas, including bespoke work for military and commercial vehicles. Our reputation for quality and reliability is the reason we’re trusted across the industry.
